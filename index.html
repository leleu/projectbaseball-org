About

The modern game of baseball is deeply informed by data. However, the steep learning curve limits how fans can engage with the sport.

This site aims to soften the learning curve, so a modern fan can run their own analysis. Run your first analysis in under 10 minutes, then continue to learn in-demand data science skills.

Data? Check.

Baseball data (fielding, xstats, transactions, game logs, PitchF/X, and more) is automatically pulled in by our army of data bots. Connect your Yahoo account, and we'll pull in your league data. Then, step-by-step, we'll teach you how to analyze your fantasy league for fun and profit.

Start Immediately

We've set up the servers and grabbed the data. You just need to practice and learn. 80% of data science is just getting the data ready (ETL) and the servers provisioned (DataOps). You'll be running your first baseball analysis in 10 minutes.

Learn by Doing

When you learn by doing, the material will seem easier as you see immediate progress. You'll build technical skills for working either in baseball or elsewhere in a data scientist role. End up with a customized league analysis of your fantasy team, built by you.
